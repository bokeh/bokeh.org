We are grateful to our fiscal sponsors, whose generous support helps ensure the sustainability of the Bokeh project.


If your company uses Bokeh and is able to sponsor the project, please contact info@bokeh.org. If you are a user who appreciates Bokeh and wants to support our mission, please consider making a donation to assist our efforts.

Bokeh was initially developed with funding from the DARPA XDATA Program, and subsequently with support from Anaconda, Inc.

Today, Bokeh is a Sponsored Project of NumFOCUS, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit charity in the United States. NumFOCUS provides Bokeh with fiscal, legal, and administrative support to help ensure the health and sustainability of the project. Visit numfocus.org for more information.

Donations to Bokeh are managed by NumFOCUS. For donors in the United States, your gift is tax-deductible to the extent provided by law. As with any donation, you should consult with your tax adviser about your particular tax situation.
